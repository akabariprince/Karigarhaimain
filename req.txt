## Section 1: Page Hero

## Section 2: For Customers Full Flow
- Heading: How It Works for Customers
- Step 1: Create Your Free Account
  - Sign up with your mobile number. Verify via WhatsApp OTP. Add your basic details and address. Done in 3 minutes.
- Step 2: Post Your Service Requirement
  - Select the service category. Describe what you need in your own words. Set your budget. Choose hourly, daily or fixed pricing. Add photos or voice notes if needed. Add your location.
- Step 3: Receive Provider Applications
  - Verified providers in your area automatically see your post. They apply with their proposed budget and timeline. You receive notifications for each application.
- Step 4: Review and Compare
  - View each provider profile. Check their ratings, completed jobs, reviews from past customers and their proposed budget. Compare easily.
- Step 5: Hire One or More
  - Select the providers you want. You can hire multiple providers for the same job if needed.
- Step 6: Make Secure Payment
  - Pay via UPI. Your payment goes into secure escrow. It is not released until you are satisfied.
- Step 7: Work Begins with OTP
  - When your provider arrives, share a one time password to officially start the contract. This protects both sides.
- Step 8: Track the Work
  - For hourly jobs, see real time clock in and clock out. Get notified when work starts and stops. For day based jobs, see daily progress.
- Step 9: Review and Approve
  - Provider submits completion photos and message. You review. If satisfied, approve and payment releases. If not, raise a concern.
- Step 10: Rate and Review
  - Leave a rating and review for your provider. Help others make better decisions.

---

## Section 3: For Providers Full Flow
- Heading: How It Works for Providers
- Step 1: Create Your Provider Account
  - Sign up with mobile. Verify via WhatsApp OTP. Add your basic details and your skill category.
- Step 2: Complete KYC Verification
  - Upload Aadhaar front and back. Enter PAN number. Add your bank account details for payouts. Submit for admin review.
- Step 3: Get Approved
  - Our team reviews your documents manually. Approval usually takes 24 to 48 hours. You get notified via WhatsApp.
- Step 4: See Nearby Jobs
  - Once approved, job requests matching your category within 50km appear on your home screen automatically.
- Step 5: Apply for Jobs
  - Read the job description. Submit your proposed budget, start time and a short message to the customer.
- Step 6: Get Hired
  - Customer reviews your profile and application. If selected, you receive a hire notification. Accept or decline.
- Step 7: Start Work with OTP
  - Customer shares an OTP. Enter it on arrival to officially begin the contract. Chat with customer for details.
- Step 8: Track and Submit
  - For hourly jobs, use clock in and clock out. For daily jobs, mark each working day. When done, submit completion photos.
- Step 9: Get Paid to Wallet
  - After customer approves, your earnings minus platform fee go to your in app wallet.
- Step 10: Withdraw Anytime
  - Request payout to your bank account anytime. Processed within 24 to 48 hours.

---

## Section 4: Payment Security Explained
- Heading: How Your Money is Protected
- Escrow System Explanation:
  - When a customer hires and pays, the money does not go directly to the provider. It is held in a secure escrow system.
  - The provider can only receive payment after the customer approves the completed work.
  - If there is a dispute, our team reviews and decides fairly.
  - Platform fee is transparent and shown upfront before payment.
- Visual flow of payment journey

## Section 5: Work Types Explained
- Heading: Three Ways to Work
- Hourly: Pay for exact hours worked. Provider clocks in and out. You see every hour tracked.
- Daily: Agree on number of days. Provider marks each working day. Simple and clear.
- Fixed Budget: Agree on a total price for the complete job. Straightforward for defined tasks.

## General FAQs
- What is this platform?
  - A marketplace that connects customers who need home services with verified local professionals. Customers post their requirements, providers apply, and work begins after secure payment.
- Is this app available across India?
  - We are currently available in select cities and expanding rapidly. Check the app for your city availability.
- Is the app free to download?
  - Yes. The app is completely free to download for both customers and providers.

---

## For Customer FAQs
- Do I need to create an account to post a service?
  - Yes. You need to verify your mobile number and create a basic profile to post a service request.
- Can I hire more than one provider for the same job?
  - Yes. For bigger jobs, you can hire multiple providers simultaneously.
- What if a provider does not show up?
  - You can cancel the contract before it starts. Payment is not released until work begins with OTP verification. Refund policy applies.
- Can I see provider details before hiring?
  - Yes. You can view their name, profile, category, experience, rating, completed jobs and reviews before making a decision.
- Is my home address visible to all providers?
  - No. Only your area is shown to providers browsing requests. Full address is only shared with providers you hire, after contract starts.

---

## For Provider FAQs
- How long does KYC approval take?
  - Typically 24 to 48 hours after document submission. You will be notified via WhatsApp.
- What happens if my KYC is rejected?
  - You will receive the rejection reason. You can resubmit corrected documents for review.
- Can I apply for multiple jobs at the same time?
  - Yes. You can apply for as many open jobs as you want in your area.
- What if a customer raises a false dispute?
  - Our team reviews all evidence including chat history, clock entries and completion photos. We protect providers from unfair claims.
- How many categories can I register under?
  - Currently one primary category per provider account.

---

## Payment FAQs
- When is payment collected from the customer?
  - Payment is collected when the customer hires and confirms the provider selection. Before work begins.
- When does the provider receive payment?
  - After the customer approves the completed work. Earnings are credited to the provider wallet.
- How do providers withdraw their earnings?
  - By requesting a payout through the app. Amount transferred to registered bank account within 24 to 48 hours.
- What is the platform fee?
  - A small percentage deducted from the provider payout. Shown clearly before every contract. No hidden charges.
- What if I need a refund?
  - Refer to our refund policy. Disputes are reviewed by our team and resolved fairly.

---

## Safety FAQs
- How are providers verified?
  - Every provider submits Aadhaar card, PAN card and bank details. Our admin team manually reviews and approves each one.
- Is my personal data safe?
  - Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties.
- What is the OTP system?
  - A one time password shared by the customer with the provider at the work location to officially start the contract. This protects both parties.
